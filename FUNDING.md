# Funding and Donations

Hands-on driver validation requires physical access to the exact device, a recovery path, storage, adapters, and enough time to repeat destructive and reboot-heavy tests safely.

## What donations may support

- Dedicated handheld test hardware
- Storage, docks, adapters, chargers, and recovery media
- Replacement parts and shipping
- Driver research and repeated end-to-end validation

Devices purchased with project funds remain available for later driver releases and future validation testing.

## What donations do not provide

A donation does not guarantee support, purchase a compatibility result, establish a deadline, create priority support, or convert the project into an official Lenovo, AMD, or Microsoft service.

Compatibility is published only after the complete workflow passes on the exact target hardware.

## Transparency

The [hardware testing tracker](docs/HARDWARE-FUNDING.md) records proposed targets and validation status. Personal donor information is not published.
