# Funding and Donations

The project can only validate a handheld that is physically available for
hands-on testing. Donations help make it possible to acquire additional
Ryzen Z1 Extreme devices and maintain the equipment needed for repeatable,
recovery-aware driver testing.

## What donations may support

Project funds may be used for:

- Purchasing additional handheld PCs for dedicated testing
- Storage devices, docks, adapters, chargers, and recovery media
- Replacement parts needed to keep test hardware operational
- Shipping, taxes, and other direct acquisition costs
- Driver research, regression testing, and release-validation expenses

Devices purchased with project funds remain project test hardware. Keeping
them available matters because later AMD releases, Windows updates, and
regressions may require the same device to be tested again.

## What a donation does not provide

A donation does not:

- Guarantee support for a requested device
- Purchase a compatibility result
- Establish a deadline or release date
- Create priority technical support
- Guarantee that a technically safe workflow is possible
- Convert the project into an official Lenovo, AMD, or Microsoft service

Compatibility will be announced only after the complete workflow has been
tested successfully on the exact model and its hardware-specific package
state.

## Device-selection process

Potential devices are considered using factors such as:

- Community interest
- Availability and acquisition cost
- Hardware similarity to an already validated platform
- Access to the device's original OEM recovery and driver packages
- Whether the device can be safely restored after failed experiments
- The likelihood that hands-on testing can produce a maintainable result

Funding a device allows the research to begin; it does not predetermine the
result.

## Transparency

The project will use
[`docs/HARDWARE-FUNDING.md`](docs/HARDWARE-FUNDING.md) to publish:

- The active or proposed hardware target
- The approximate acquisition goal
- Whether the device has been acquired
- The current research and validation stage
- Whether support was released, paused, or found impractical

Exact personal donor information will not be published. Platform fees,
taxes, shipping, and price changes may affect the amount available for a
purchase.

project's behalf unless this repository is updated to identify them.

## Independence

Donations do not create affiliation with or endorsement by Lenovo, AMD,
Microsoft, GitHub, or any handheld manufacturer.
