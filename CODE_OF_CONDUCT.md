# Community Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and safety-focused.

Contributors and users must not harass, threaten, demean, or discriminate against others. Technical disagreement is welcome; personal attacks are not.

When discussing failures:

- Critique code and evidence, not people.
- Avoid pressuring users to bypass safety checks.
- Do not shame users for stopping a risky operation.
- Clearly separate verified results from speculation.
- Respect requests to remove personal data from logs or screenshots.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.
